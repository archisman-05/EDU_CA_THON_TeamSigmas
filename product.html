Medicinal Plants

Explore the world of medicinal powers!

Turmeric

Scientific Name: Curcuma Longa

Medicinal Properties: Anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, antimicrobial

Diseases It Treats: Arthritis, digestive issues, skin conditions, and infections.

Geographic Location: Native to Southeast Asia, particularly India.

Neem

Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica

Medicinal Properties: Antibacterial, antifungal, anti-inflammatory.

Diseases It Treats: Skin conditions (eczema, acne), infections, digestive problems.

Geographic Location: Native to India and other tropical regions.

Brahmi

Scientific Name: Bacopa monnieri

Medicinal Properties: Cognitive enhancer, anti-anxiety, antioxidant.

Diseases It Treats: Memory loss, stress, anxiety, and cognitive decline.

Geographic Location: Native to India, found in wetlands and marshy areas.

Ashwagandha

Scientific Name: Withania somnifera

Medicinal Properties: Adaptogenic, anti-inflammatory, stress-relieving.

Diseases It Treats: Anxiety, stress, fatigue, insomnia, and cognitive decline.

Geographic Location: Native to India, North Africa, and the Middle East.

Amla

Scientific Name: Emblica officinalis

Medicinal Properties: High in vitamin C, antioxidant, rejuvenating.

Diseases It Treats: Immunity booster, digestive issues, skin health.

Geographic Location: Native to India, Southeast Asia.

Modern Medicinal Plants

Modern plants with a powerful support!

Moringa

Scientific Name: Moringa oleifera

Known as the "drumstick tree," it has a wide range of medicinal benefits, including boosting immunity, reducing inflammation, and controlling blood sugar levels.

Geographic Location: Northwest India, Pakistan, and Nepal. It is also cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions across the world.

Ginseng

Scientific Name: Panax ginseng

Used in traditional medicine for boosting energy, reducing stress, improving mental clarity, and enhancing immune function.

Geographic Location: Eastern Asia, primarily found in Korea, China, and Russia (Siberia). American ginseng (Panax quinquefolius) is native to North America.

Soursop

Scientific Name: Annona muricata

The fruit and leaves are used to treat cancer, boost immunity, reduce inflammation, and support digestive health.

Geographic Location: ETropical Americas, particularly found in Central America, Caribbean, and parts of South America. It is also grown in tropical regions worldwide.

Aparajita

Scientific Name: Clitoria Ternatea

Aparajita flower is known for its nootropic effects, enhancing memory, focus, and mental clarity. It is often used in traditional medicine to improve brain function.

Geographic Location: Tropical Asia, particularly in India, Sri Lanka, Thailand, and Southeast Asia.

Dragon’s Blood

Scientific Name: Daemonorops draco

The resin from this tree is used for its wound-healing properties, anti-inflammatory effects, and to treat digestive issues.

Geographic Location: Southeast Asia, particularly in Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. It is also found in parts of India and Africa.
